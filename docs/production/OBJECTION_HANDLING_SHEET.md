# BlackChain Objection Handling Sheet

## “This sounds operationally heavy.”

Response:

- the current launch path is intentionally structured
- deployment, verification, onboarding, and support are already documented
- the model is designed for repeatable execution rather than ad hoc rollout

## “Why the hardware tiers?”

Response:

- the plan tiers are aligned to simple issuance:
  - Standard -> Pi Zero
  - Premium -> Pi 3
  - Family/Business -> Pi 4
- the mapping keeps launch packaging clear and consistent

## “What proves this can launch?”

Response:

- launch and readiness materials are in place
- pilot proof, operator workflows, and support templates are documented
- the current path emphasizes verification and rollback readiness

## “What if rollout hits issues?”

Response:

- support, triage, escalation, and rollback materials already exist
- operators follow a defined deployment and monitoring path
