# Bring BlackChain To Your Town

BlackChain is designed for repeatable town-by-town rollout with a clear launch path, local host sites, and named operators.

## What This Means

- a local host site can support deployment
- a local or assigned operator can activate and verify service
- launch materials, onboarding, and support paths are already defined

## Launch Path

- identify a town opportunity
- align a host site
- assign an operator
- activate the selected plan tier
