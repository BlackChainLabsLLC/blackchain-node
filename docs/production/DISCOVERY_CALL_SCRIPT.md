# BlackChain Discovery Call Script

## Opening

- thanks for taking the call
- our goal is to understand launch fit, host-site fit, and operator path for your territory or site

## Discovery Questions

- what town, territory, or site are you focused on
- do you already have a host-site candidate
- do you have an operator or technical owner in mind
- which plan tier seems closest to your expected use
- what timing are you considering for activation

## Qualification

- host-site readiness
- operator readiness
- support readiness
- launch timing

## Close

- summarize fit
- identify blockers
- agree next step
