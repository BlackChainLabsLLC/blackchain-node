# BlackChain Customer Launch FAQ

## What is BlackChain?

BlackChain is a node-based launch path built for local rollout with operator support and plan-tier hardware alignment.

## How does launch work?

A host site is prepared, an operator provisions and verifies the deployment, and the customer is onboarded through a defined activation path.

## What plans are available?

- Standard
- Premium
- Family/Business

## What hardware comes with each plan?

- Standard -> Pi Zero
- Premium -> Pi 3
- Family/Business -> Pi 4

## Who helps after launch?

Named operators and support contacts handle activation, verification, monitoring, and follow-up.
