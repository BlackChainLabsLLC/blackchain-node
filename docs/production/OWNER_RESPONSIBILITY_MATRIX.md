# BlackChain Owner/Responsibility Matrix

| Area | Primary Owner | Supporting Owner | Main Artifacts |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| launch decision | founder or launch owner | operator owner | launch brief, scorecard, review agenda |
| deployment and verification | operator owner | support owner | manifest, promote/rollback, smoke, field verify |
| customer activation | operator owner | support owner | activation checklist, onboarding sheet |
| territory pipeline | founder or sales owner | operator owner | territory brief, pipeline tracker, discovery template |
| support and incidents | support owner | operator owner | triage template, response macros, escalation matrix |
| website and campaigns | marketing or builder owner | founder | website copy, build handoff, QA checklist |
